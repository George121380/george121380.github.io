Research

My long-term research goal is to build human-like intelligence that can continually learn from experience and apply them for reasoning and planning in the physical world. The central theme of my research is to abstract reusable knowledge from data (internet, simulation, and embodied interaction data) and translate it into structured representations (scene maps, programs, affordance maps) that enhance model capability and enable more efficient task execution.
